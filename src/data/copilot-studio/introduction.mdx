## Environments, Solutions & Licensing

### Environments

<div className="grid grid-cols-3 gap-4 my-4">

  <div className="mode-pill-card">
    <div className="mode-pill-card-title">
      <Icon icon="fluent:globe-shield-24-regular" height={22} />
      <strong>What is an Environment?</strong>
    </div>
    <p className="text-sm m-0">An isolated container in Power Platform with its own Dataverse database, users, roles, and resources. Think of it as a <em>namespace</em> for all your apps, flows, and copilots.</p>
  </div>

  <div className="mode-pill-card">
    <div className="mode-pill-card-title">
      <Icon icon="fluent:arrow-move-24-regular" height={22} />
      <strong>Dev → Test → Prod</strong>
    </div>
    <p className="text-sm m-0">Best practice: maintain separate environments for development, testing, and production. Copilots and their dependencies are migrated between them using <strong>Solutions</strong>.</p>
  </div>

  <div className="mode-pill-card">
    <div className="mode-pill-card-title">
      <Icon icon="fluent:person-settings-24-regular" height={22} />
      <strong>Environment Types</strong>
    </div>
    <ul className="list-arrow m-0 text-sm">
      <li><strong>Default</strong> — shared sandbox, every tenant has one</li>
      <li><strong>Sandbox</strong> — for dev &amp; test; can be reset</li>
      <li><strong>Production</strong> — live workloads, backed up</li>
      <li><strong>Developer</strong> — personal, free, 1 GB Dataverse</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

</div>

### Solutions

<div className="mode-pill-card my-4">
  <div className="mode-pill-card-title">
    <Icon icon="fluent:box-24-regular" height={22} />
    <strong>Solutions are deployment packages</strong>
  </div>
  <ul className="list-arrow m-0">
    <li>A solution bundles a copilot together with its flows, environment variables, connection references, and security roles.</li>
    <li><strong>Managed solutions</strong> are locked (for production) — changes must come through the pipeline.</li>
    <li><strong>Unmanaged solutions</strong> are editable (for development).</li>
    <li>Export as a <code>.zip</code> and import into another environment, or use <strong>Power Platform Pipelines</strong> / Azure DevOps for automated ALM.</li>
    <li>Always build copilots <em>inside a solution</em> — copilots created outside solutions cannot be easily migrated.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
